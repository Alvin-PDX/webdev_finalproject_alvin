Connect 4

Welcome to Connect 4! This project was created using Node.js, socket.io, and Express.

Join a room on the list in the right or create a room using the button below.

    Something's not right...

    When it's your turn, click on a column to place a piece of your color there.

    The first player to get four pieces of their color in a line horizontally, vertically, or diagonally wins.

    Refresh the page to leave the room or forfeit.
